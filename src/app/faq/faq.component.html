About MyCryptoChamp

  • App based on Ethereum blockchain technology.
  • Game about upgrading your champs by fighting, getting better items and trading them. The best 800 champs are daily rewarded by Ether.

Getting started

  • A browser (Chrome, Firefox or Opera) with installed MetaMask plugin.
  • Metamask is used as a gateway, which connects Ethereum blokchain and your browser.
  • For iOS also can be used browser Cipher.
  • You will need to install MetaMask
  • Make sure you do not forget your password or the seed words, since it is only one way to access your funds.
  • Install MetaMask
  • This happens when you haven’t logged in to MetaMask since you opened browser.
  • To unlock simply click on the MetaMask extension in right top corner and type in your password.
  • U.S. citizens only: you can buy Ether (ETH) in MetaMask. ETH is a cryptocurrency that enables MyCryptoChamp to run.
  • Everyone else: you will need to purchase ETH from an exchange. The easiest way is using Coinbase and then transferring the ETH from your Coinbase wallet to your MetaMask wallet.
  • Unfortunately, you cannot play MyCryptoChamp with a Coinbase wallet or any other wallets.
  • You cannot use USD/EUR to play MyCryptoChamp — all currencies need to be converted into ETH first.
  • U.S. citizens only: you are able to purchase ETH directly from the MetaMask wallet using the Coinbase widget. This is more convenient and doesn’t require you to create two accounts.
  • Everyone else: you need to buy ETH from an exchange using fiat currency. Coinbase is the easiest to setup however the choice is ultimately up to you.
    Copy your MetaMask address by clicking on the “...” then “Copy Address to clipboard”. Go to Coinbase and click “Accounts” and select your ETH Wallet and then click “send”. Paste the MetaMask address in the box with the amount you’d like to transfer.
  • ‘Gas’ is a shorthand used to describe the cost of powering a transaction or contract in Ethereum, which is the blockchain network that MyCryptoChamp is built on.
  • Because blockchain is decentralized, every transaction is distributed through multiple computers, not a central server. This ensures each called operation is secure. However, it also takes more computational power, which is covered by the cost of gas. Ethereum typically communicates this cost in gwei (1 million gwei = 1 ether/ETH).

Beginners Guide

  • To win a fight, you need to have higher attack power than target’s defence power.
  • After every fight winner is rewarded with ability points that are randomly divided into attack power and defence power.
  • However, you lose some when you are defeated in the fight.
  • The bigger the difference, the lower is the amount of received points.
  • By defeating champs you can overtake their place in leaderboard.
  • Every created champ gets random ability points.
  • Min attack power: 3
  • Min defence power: 2
  • Max and min cooldown time: 24h and 16h
  • Equipping items makes your champ stronger what increases chances to win a fight.
  • New items are generated by opening loot boxes.

 In order to create a better item, you can forge two items together.
  • Players can freely trade their champs and items in the market.
  • 1% commission fee is applied on every sale.
  • Top 800 champs are daily rewarded in Ether.
  • When a new champ or loot box are sold, the half of it goes to players.
  • The first champ in the leaderboard gets 0.2%.
  • 0.0002% is subtracted from every next position.
